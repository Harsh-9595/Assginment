Leonardo da Vinci

Biography

Leonardo da Vinci, was renaissance polymath known for his artistic masterpieces and ingenious inventions.Born in Vinci, Italy, in 1452, he made significant contributions to various fields, including art, sience and engineering.

Da Vinci's works include the Mona Lisa, The Last Supper, and detailed anatomical sketches. His innovative designs for fliying machines and war instruments reflect his diverse interests and curiosity.
